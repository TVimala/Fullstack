Chennai

Chennai is a major administrative and cultural center, and a key center for India's economy, education, and culture. It's also known as the "Gateway to South India". Chennai is a popular tourist destination in southern India. It's home to ancient temples, vibrant arts, natural wonders, and a bustling culinary scene. Chennai is also known as the "Detroit of India" because of its automobile industry, which produces more than 40% of India's auto parts and motor vehicles.
